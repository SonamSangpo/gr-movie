The Timeless Legend of the Life of Guru Rinpoche has been fully translated and digitaly remastered to be presented to an international audience The Life of Guru Rinpoche is a Namdrolling Monastery Production presented by The Penor Rinpoche Charity Foundation in association with Pathgate Institute, produced by HH Penor Norbu Rinpoche and remastered by Venerable Lama Dondrup Dorje under the Blessings of HH Penor Norbu Rinpoche.
